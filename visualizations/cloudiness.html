Latitude vs. Max Cloudiness

This scatterplot measures the relationship between a city's latitude and its max cloudiness.

Taking a look here, there seems to be a relatively weak correlation between a city's latitude and its cloudiness; however, there do seem to be more extremes between high and low wind speeds as latitude increases.
