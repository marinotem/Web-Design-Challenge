Latitude vs. Wind

Here is a scatterplot measuring the relationship between a city's latitude and its wind speed.

As latitude increases, it seems there might be some correlation to increased windspeed. However, this increase in wind speed is not extremely strong as many cities at lower and higher latitudes seem to more closely cluster around the lower wind speeds.
